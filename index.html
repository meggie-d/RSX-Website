WE ARE A UNIVERSITY OF TORONTO DESIGN TEAM PUSHING THE BOUNDS OF ROBOTICS IN SPACE EXPLORATION

Needless to say, space exploration is unbelievably exciting and challenging. The goal of RSX is to ensure its members gain a better understanding of the practical side of space engineering. By designing, building, and testing robots designed for planetary and lunar exploration, RSX competes in space engineering competitions around the world. At heart, our true passion is manned space exploration. Our overarching vision will always be for robots to work with humans, not instead of them, and pave the way for manned exploration. We also aim to spark interest in robotics and STEM through outreach events at schools and within the community. Please join us on this stellar adventure!

WHAT WE DO

INNOVATE

At RSX, our primary focus is pushing the boundaries of science, technology and engineering to create the next generation of robotics for space exploration.

EDUCATE

We take pride in promoting STEM education amongst youth, and in inspiring the next generation of engineers to undertake the challenges of space exploration. We hold also hold two mini robotics hackathons each year.

COMPETE

RSX proudly represents the University of Toronto at various international competitions, including the University Rover Challenge and the CanSat competition.
